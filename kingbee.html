Welcome

King Bee Chinese Restaurant that serves authentic Chinese Cuisine originated in Brgy. Manggahan, General Trias, province of Cavite on August 17, 2001. The restaurant has a wide array of various Chinese recipes such as dimsums, roast specialties, noodles soup and other main courses cooked in authentic way. The company has hired the expertise of International Chefs from Hong Kong who personally prepare the menu and manage the kitchen and commissary. The operation is being managed by best experienced professionals in the field of food services.

The company was able to create awareness and clientele relationship among the business communities of: GATEWAY BUSINESS PARK, FIRST CAVITE INDUSTRIAL ESTATE and among local residents of General Trias, Trece Martirez, Dasmarinas, Cavite, part of Amadeo and other nearby towns. To top it all, even the golfers of Eagle Ridge Residential Estates, where you can find the biggest golf course in the Philippines, look forward to dine in our place after an enjoyable game.

The company never gets startled among competitors but still can manage to establish the edge among existing food businesses in the area. This is through efforts of maintaining high-quality products and services that have created a remarkable patronage for the company. As time goes by, King Bee not only caters for walk-in clients but it also reaches at the door steps of each residence near our vicinity who wished to have our food products by delivery. This is because we want everyone to experience the delightful treat of our food products, and at the same time to satisfy the needs of our clients. We even also accommodate off-premise catering services in order to be more accessible to those clients who may find hard time of reaching our location. It was also a big surprise when most clients give us a phone call just to make a reservation for them before they reach our area.

We entrust that our expertise in food and continuous effort in creating new ideas and concepts to expand the business that will provide the community with excellent dining experience at the most affordable cost. It also envisions of becoming a leading Chinese food experts in the country. Our patrons are the foremost inspiration why the company never stops to aim high. This is because of the patronage and positive reception with the foods and services we provide. Therefore, expansion of its kind is always the thrust of venture by the aggressive men and women behind KING BEE FOOD CORPORATION.
